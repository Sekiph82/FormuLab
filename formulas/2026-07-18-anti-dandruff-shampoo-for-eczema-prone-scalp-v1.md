# Formulation Card: Botanical Soothing Anti-Dandruff Cleanser

**Purpose:** A gentle, sulfate-free anti-dandruff shampoo utilizing Piroctone Olamine and soothing botanicals for eczema-prone scalps in hard water environments.

## How It Works

### Hard Water Management
Tetrasodium Glutamate Diacetate effectively sequesters calcium and magnesium ions prevalent in Kenyan borehole water, preventing surfactant precipitation and ensuring consistent lather.

### Scalp Barrier Support
The combination of alkyl polyglucosides and sarcosinates provides effective cleansing without disrupting the lipid barrier, critical for eczema-prone skin.

## What to Avoid
- ❌ Sulfates (SLS/SLES) — Known to be harsh and strip natural oils, exacerbating eczema and scalp sensitivity.
- ❌ Fragrance — High risk of contact dermatitis in individuals with eczema-prone scalps.

## Usage
1. Apply to wet hair and scalp
2. Massage gently for 2-3 minutes to allow active ingredients to work
3. Rinse thoroughly with clean water

## ⚠️ Warnings
- For external use only
- Avoid contact with eyes
- Discontinue use if irritation occurs
- Evidence-based candidate, not a validated commercial product; lab validation required.
