# Formulation Card: Mild Glucoside Anti-Dandruff Wash

**Purpose:** A minimalist, ultra-mild shampoo designed for sensitive, eczema-prone scalps in hard water regions.

**References:** Wiley 2020 (DOI:10.1111/pde.14112); Elsevier 2022 (DOI:10.1016/j.colsurfb.2022.113012)

## How It Works

### Mild Cleansing
Uses a blend of non-ionic glucosides and mild isethionates to cleanse without stripping the skin barrier.

### Hard Water Buffering
Trisodium Citrate acts as both a chelator for hard water and a buffer to maintain the pH in the 4.5-5.5 range, ideal for scalp health.

## What to Avoid
- ❌ Parabens — Excluded to meet clean beauty standards and minimize skin irritation.
- ❌ Synthetic Fragrance — Avoided to prevent allergic reactions on compromised scalp skin.

## Usage
1. Wet hair
2. Apply to scalp
3. Lather gently
4. Rinse thoroughly

## ⚠️ Warnings
- Contains Salicylic Acid
- Not for use on children under 3 years
- Evidence-based candidate, not a validated commercial product; lab validation required.
