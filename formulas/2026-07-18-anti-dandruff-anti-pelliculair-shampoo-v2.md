# Formulation Card: Botanical Glucoside Clarifying Shampoo

**Purpose:** A gentle, plant-derived cleansing system designed to remove mineral buildup while soothing the scalp with Aloe Vera.

**References:** Journal of Surfactants and Detergents 2018 (DOI:10.1002/jsde.12157)

## How It Works

### Chelation via Citrate
Sodium Citrate acts as a mild chelating agent, preventing the formation of soap scum and mineral deposits on the hair shaft.

### Dandruff Mitigation
Zinc Pyrithione is a standard, effective treatment for scalp flaking, working by inhibiting fungal growth.

## What to Avoid
- ❌ Formaldehyde Donors — Known allergens and sensitizers.
- ❌ Parfum — Eliminated to ensure safety for sensitive skin.

## Usage
1. Wet hair thoroughly
2. Apply shampoo to roots
3. Rinse with clean water

## ⚠️ Warnings
- Shake well before use
- Keep out of reach of children
- Evidence-based candidate, not a validated commercial product; lab validation required.
