# Formulation Card: Ultra-Mild Glucoside Baby Shampoo

**Purpose:** A minimalist, high-tolerance cleansing formula designed for sensitive infant scalps using non-ionic surfactants.

**References:** Loffredo et al. 2012 (DOI:10.1155/2012/495917)

## How It Works

### Barrier-Respecting Cleansing
Utilizes alkyl polyglucosides which are non-ionic surfactants known for minimal protein denaturation and low irritation potential compared to traditional sulfates, preserving the stratum corneum integrity (Loffredo et al., 2012).

## What to Avoid
- ❌ Sulfates — High potential for skin barrier disruption and irritation on delicate infant skin.
- ❌ Fragrance — Common allergen and irritant for sensitive or atopic-prone infant skin.

## Usage
1. Apply a small amount to wet hair
2. Gently massage into scalp
3. Rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water

## ⚠️ Warnings
- For external use only
- Avoid direct contact with eyes
- Evidence-based candidate, not a validated commercial product; lab validation required.
