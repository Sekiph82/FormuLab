# Formulation Card: Isethionate-Betaine Conditioning Shampoo

**Purpose:** A gentle, conditioning baby shampoo utilizing amphoteric and acyl isethionate surfactants for a creamy, non-stripping lather.

**References:** Kaur et al. 2024 (DOI:10.38212/2224-6614.3526)

## How It Works

### Synergistic Mild Cleansing
The combination of Sodium Lauroyl Methyl Isethionate and Cocamidopropyl Betaine creates a mild, high-foaming system that is significantly less irritating than traditional anionic surfactants, suitable for frequent use (Kaur et al., 2024).

## What to Avoid
- ❌ Formaldehyde Donors — Prohibited due to potential sensitization and toxicity concerns in infants.
- ❌ SLS/SLES — Excluded to maintain the integrity of the infant skin barrier.

## Usage
1. Wet hair thoroughly
2. Massage into hair and scalp
3. Rinse well

## ⚠️ Warnings
- Keep out of reach of children
- If irritation occurs, discontinue use
- Evidence-based candidate, not a validated commercial product; lab validation required.
