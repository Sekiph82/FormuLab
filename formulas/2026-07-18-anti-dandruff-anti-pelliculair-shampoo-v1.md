## How It Works

### Hard Water Management
Tetrasodium Glutamate Diacetate effectively chelates calcium and magnesium ions prevalent in Kenyan borehole water, preventing surfactant precipitation and enhancing foam stability.

### Microbial Control
Piroctone Olamine is a highly effective, non-irritating antifungal agent that targets Malassezia species, the primary cause of dandruff.

## What to Avoid
- ❌ Sulfates — Causes excessive stripping of natural oils in Afro-textured hair and potential scalp irritation.
- ❌ Fragrance — High risk of contact dermatitis on sensitive, dandruff-prone scalps.

## Usage
1. Apply to wet hair
2. Massage into scalp for 3 minutes
3. Rinse thoroughly

## ⚠️ Warnings
