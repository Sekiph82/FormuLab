# Formulation Card: Biosurfactant Eco-White Powder

**Purpose:** Eco-friendly formulation using microbial biosurfactants for effective oil removal and fabric brightening.

**References:** S. R. H. A. 2017 (DOI:10.1002/elsc.201700152)

## Formulation Table

| # | Ingredient (INCI) | Function | Weight % |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Sodium Sulfate | Filler | 45.0 |

| 3 | Sodium Tripolyphosphate | Builder | 20.0 |
| 4 | Sodium Carbonate | pH Adjuster | 15.0 |
| 5 | Sodium Percarbonate | Bleaching Agent | 8.0 |
| 6 | Optical Brightener | Whitening Agent | 0.5 |

## How It Works

### Biosurfactant Efficacy
Biosurfactants derived from B. subtilis provide excellent oil emulsification and are highly biodegradable.

### Builder Synergy
STPP sequesters calcium and magnesium ions, preventing soil redeposition on white fabrics.

## What to Avoid
- ❌ Synthetic Fragrances — To maintain a hypoallergenic profile for sensitive skin.

## Usage
1. Add to the detergent dispenser.
2. Suitable for all wash temperatures; best results at 40C.

## ⚠️ Warnings
- Check local regulations regarding phosphate content in detergents.
- Keep container tightly closed.
- Evidence-based candidate, not a validated commercial product; lab validation required.
