# Formulation Card: Enzymatic Bio-Bright Powder

**Purpose:** Advanced bio-detergent featuring alkaline proteases for superior protein stain removal on white laundry.

**References:** M. A. H. A. A. A. A. 2019 (DOI:10.1155/2019/6470897)

## How It Works

### Enzymatic Digestion
Alkaline proteases break down protein-based stains (blood, grass) which are common on white fabrics.

### Synergistic Cleaning
Surfactants emulsify lipids while enzymes target specific soil types at pH 9-10.

## What to Avoid
- ❌ Chlorine Bleach — Will deactivate the enzymes and reduce cleaning efficacy.

## Usage
1. Use in standard top or front-loading machines.
2. Ensure water temperature is between 30C and 50C for enzyme activity.

## ⚠️ Warnings
- Enzymes may cause sensitization; avoid inhalation of powder dust.
- Store in a cool, dry place to maintain enzyme stability.
- Evidence-based candidate, not a validated commercial product; lab validation required.
