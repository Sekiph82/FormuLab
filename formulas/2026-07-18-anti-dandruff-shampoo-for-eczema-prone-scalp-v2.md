## How It Works

### Sebum Control
Zinc PCA helps regulate sebum production, which is often a trigger for Malassezia growth associated with dandruff.

### Chelation Efficiency
Disodium EDTA protects the surfactant system from hard water ions, ensuring the formula remains stable and effective in high-mineral water.

## What to Avoid
- ❌ Formaldehyde Donors — Common preservatives like DMDM Hydantoin are sensitizing and unsuitable for eczema-prone skin.
- ❌ MI/MCI — Strong allergens that pose a high risk for sensitive scalp users.

## Usage
1. Apply to scalp
2. Massage thoroughly
3. Leave for 3 minutes
4. Rinse well

## ⚠️ Warnings
- Keep out of reach of children
- If product enters eyes, rinse with water
