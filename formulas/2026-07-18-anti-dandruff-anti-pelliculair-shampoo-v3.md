# Formulation Card: Sulfosuccinate Scalp-Balancing Shampoo

**Purpose:** A high-performance, mild cleansing formula using Disodium Laureth Sulfosuccinate for superior foam in hard water environments.

**References:** International Journal of Cosmetic Science 2019 (DOI:10.1111/ics.12560)

## How It Works

### Hard Water Tolerance
Disodium Laureth Sulfosuccinate is significantly less sensitive to water hardness than traditional sulfates, maintaining high foam volume.

### Exfoliation
Salicylic acid aids in the removal of dead skin cells from the scalp, reducing the appearance of dandruff flakes.

## What to Avoid
- ❌ Methylisothiazolinone — Common allergen, excluded for safety.
- ❌ SLS/SLES — Too harsh for Afro-textured hair and sensitive scalps.

## Usage
1. Apply to scalp
2. Leave for 2 minutes
3. Rinse thoroughly

## ⚠️ Warnings
- Avoid contact with eyes
- Discontinue if irritation occurs
- Evidence-based candidate, not a validated commercial product; lab validation required.
