# Formulation Card: BrightWhite Mineral-Builder Powder

**Purpose:** High-performance alkaline powder for white fabrics utilizing sodium percarbonate for bleaching and zeolites for water softening.

**References:** K. H. Tan 2007 (DOI:10.5650/jos.56.327)

## How It Works

### Alkaline Cleaning
Sodium carbonate maintains pH 10-11, facilitating the saponification of fatty soils on cotton fibers.

### Oxidative Whitening
Sodium percarbonate releases hydrogen peroxide in water, effectively oxidizing organic stains on white fabrics.

## What to Avoid
- ❌ Colored dyes — Formula contains high levels of bleaching agents that would damage non-white fabrics.

## Usage
1. Add 50g of powder to the main wash compartment.
2. Select a wash cycle with at least 40C water temperature for optimal percarbonate activation.

## ⚠️ Warnings
- Keep away from moisture to prevent premature percarbonate decomposition.
- Do not use on wool or silk.
- Evidence-based candidate, not a validated commercial product; lab validation required.
